The Best Movies According to Tshaps

My top 3 movies of all-time

Gladiator

Gladiator is very much about love at its core, even though it’s wrapped in themes of revenge and power. Love drives the actions of many characters and gives the story its emotional depth.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) is a profound story about patience, resilience, and the enduring hope that drives a person forward even in the darkest of circumstances. The theme of patience is woven throughout the film as we follow the journey of Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) over two decades in Shawshank Prison.

The Matrix

The Matrix, is fundamentally a story about the power of the mind and the ability to reshape reality through belief, perception, and self-awareness. It delves deeply into themes of human potential, free will, and the mind’s role in defining what is real.
